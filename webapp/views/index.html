ZKDonate is

ZKDonate is a zero knowledge proof based donation application designed to enhance the security and privacy of charitable contributions. With this application, donors can make donations without revealing any personal or financial information, thus reducing the risk of identity theft and fraud.

About Us

We are the team behind ZKDonate, a revolutionary donation application designed to provide a more secure and private way for individuals to make charitable contributions. Our goal is to leverage the power of zero knowledge proofs to ensure that donors can give to their favorite causes without the fear of exposing sensitive information.

Our team is made up of those who share a common vision of making the world a better place through technology. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to give back to their community and support causes they care about, without worrying about the risks of identity theft or fraud.

We are excited to be participating in the ETHTokyo Hackathon, and we look forward to showcasing our innovative solution to the judges and fellow participants. With ZKDonate, we hope to inspire others to explore the possibilities of zero knowledge proofs and to join us in creating a safer, more secure world for everyone.
